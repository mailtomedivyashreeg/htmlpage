Skills

The Most Important to develop to succeed int the life

  • Concentration
  • Voice
  • Communication
  • Creativity
  • Interest

Concentration

We live in a world of distractions.the ability to concentaret on the single task is one of the most important skills to develop if you want to create anything of value.

voice

A powerful physical voice is one that connects through the whole body of the speaker, down to the toes and out to the fingertips and then out into the world with good posture and full breaths

Communication

the methods that are used for travelling to and from a place or for sending messages between places.

Creativity

Creativity is defined as the tendency to generate or recognize ideas, alternatives, or possibilities that may be useful in solving problems, communicating with others, and entertaining ourselves and others.

Interest

a desire to learn or hear more about somebody/something or to be involved with somebody/something
